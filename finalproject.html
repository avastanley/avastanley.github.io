Need to relax or have some fun? Use our coloring book that my partner and I coded from scratch!

How to play: Use your trackpad or mouse to click and hold to color. If no color is chosen, black will automatically be drawn.
To chose a different color, use the palette provided and click which color you would like to use. Once clicked, go ahead and hold down to draw.
Feel free to switch between each color. Once finished with a page, click the "Next" button in the top right of the screen.
When finished with all the pages you will be brought to the end screen, to restart the game, simply press the space bar. Happy coloring!
